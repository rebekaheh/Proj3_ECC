Always Have The Right Ingredients On Hand

If you are new to cooking, building your pantry is a critical step that can facilitate your weekly cooking with ingredients on hand. We at Anyone Can Cook want you to make buildintg your pantry easy so that you don't end up buying ingredients thatyou will rarely use!

To help with this, we reviewed hundreds of recipes to identify the most popular ingredients you should always have on hand, in addition to the most popular ingredients by type of cuisine. Our review included recipes from Chinese, African, Italian, Thai, Latin American, and American Southern cuisines.

The following ingredients are staples to keep on hand, no matter your preferences, so you can always throw something together! These are the most common recipe ingredients, in no particular order:

  • Salt
  • Garlic
  • Pepper
  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Olive Oil
  • Vegetable Oil/Cooking Spray
  • Chicken/Vegetable Stock/Broth
  • Onions (Yellow/White)
  • Chicken (Breast/Thigh)
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Milk
